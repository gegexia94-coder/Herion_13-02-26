# Herion - Digital Accountant Platform

## Architecture
Frontend: React + Tailwind + Shadcn | Backend: FastAPI + MongoDB | Auth: Cookie JWT | AI: GPT-5.2 via Emergent Key | Brand: Geometric H #0ABFCF

## Product Vision
Herion is a digital accountant experience for Italian and international users. Free access, guides through official fiscal/administrative procedures, tracks real progress, protects from incomplete logic, and supports cross-border document needs.
